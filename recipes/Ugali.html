UGALI RECIPES

MAKE THE BEST UGALI!

This recipe is very easy to put together and actually makes the perfect side dish.
It can be served with so many dishes and it is made using maize flour to a type of mush

It is popular in most african countries with many different names. It has a neutral taste similar
unflavored popcorn, due to this it can be served with soup, stew or vegetables.It has a lot of vitamins and minerals

Ingredients

  • Coarse cornmeal (maize flour)
  • Water

Steps to make Ugali

  1. Boil water in a pot
  2. Add cornmeal, a little at a time and stir
  3. Continue stirring, the mixture will form a dough-like consistency as it cooks. Break any lumps that form by pressing them on the sides of your pot.
  4. Shape the ugali, remove it from the heat, and transfer it to a plate and serve.
